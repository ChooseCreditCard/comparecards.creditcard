Key things to compare

Every comparison table on Choose.Creditcard focuses on a few core dimensions:

Dimension What to look for
Annual fee Is the fee justified by the rewards, perks and insurance?
Rewards structure Flat cashback vs. categories (travel, groceries, fuel, etc.).
Foreign transaction fees 0% is preferred if you travel or shop in other currencies.
Credit requirements What kind of credit history does the card typically require?
Travel perks Lounge, insurance, fast track, hotel and airline partners.
Upgrade path Can you move to a better card after 6–18 months of good use?

How comparisons are made

Full comparison tables live on Choose.Creditcard. They follow a consistent methodology:

  • • Separation of editorial rankings and affiliate placements
  • • Clear explanation of fees, limitations and risks
  • • Card data structured for side-by-side viewing
  • • Focus on real-world use, not just headline bonuses
